# 2. Propagation of Error II

The measured length and width are:

$$
L = (15.3 \pm 0.1)\text{ cm}
$$

$$
W = (8.4 \pm 0.1)\text{ cm}
$$

The area of a rectangular plate is:

$$
A = LW
$$

$$
A = (15.3)(8.4)
$$

$$
A = 128.52\text{ cm}^{2}
$$

For multiplication, the relative uncertainty is:

$$
\frac{\Delta A}{A}
=
\sqrt{
\left(\frac{\Delta L}{L}\right)^{2}
+
\left(\frac{\Delta W}{W}\right)^{2}
}
$$

Substitute the values:

$$
\Delta A =
128.52
\sqrt{
\left(\frac{0.1}{15.3}\right)^{2}
+
\left(\frac{0.1}{8.4}\right)^{2}
}
$$

$$
\Delta A = 1.75\text{ cm}^{2}
$$

Final answer:

$$
\boxed{A = (128.5 \pm 1.7)\text{ cm}^{2}}
$$
