# 1. Propagation of Error I

Given:

\[
r = (6.20 \pm 0.05)\text{ cm}
\]

Volume of a sphere:

\[
V = \frac{4}{3}\pi r^3
\]

Substitute the value of the radius:

\[
V = \frac{4}{3}\pi(6.20)^3
\]

\[
V = 998.31\text{ cm}^3
\]

Now calculate the uncertainty.

For a sphere:

\[
\Delta V = 4\pi r^2 \Delta r
\]

Substitute the values:

\[
\Delta V = 4\pi(6.20)^2(0.05)
\]

\[
\Delta V = 24.15\text{ cm}^3
\]

Final result:

\[
\boxed{V = (998 \pm 24)\text{ cm}^3}
\]
