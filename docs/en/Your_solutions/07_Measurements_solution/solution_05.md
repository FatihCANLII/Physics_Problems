# 5. Percentage Calculation

The measured time is:

$$
t = 5.45 \pm 0.22\text{ s}
$$

The percentage uncertainty is calculated using:

$$
\%\text{ uncertainty}
=
\frac{\Delta t}{t}\times100
$$

Substitute the values into the formula:

$$
\%\text{ uncertainty}
=
\frac{0.22}{5.45}\times100
$$

$$
\%\text{ uncertainty}
=
4.04\%
$$

Final answer:

$$
\boxed{4.0\%}
$$
