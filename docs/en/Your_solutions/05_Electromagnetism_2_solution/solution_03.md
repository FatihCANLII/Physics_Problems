# 3. Biot–Savart Law

## Problem

A small wire segment of length `0.1 m` carries a current of `3 A`.

The segment is `0.2 m` away from point `P`.

Calculate the magnetic field at point `P`.

Assume the wire segment is perpendicular to the line connecting it to point `P`.

---

## Formula

Biot–Savart Law:

\[
B = \frac{\mu_0}{4\pi}
\frac{I \Delta l \sin\theta}{r^2}
\]

---

## Given Values

\[
I = 3 \, \text{A}
\]

\[
\Delta l = 0.1 \, \text{m}
\]

\[
r = 0.2 \, \text{m}
\]

\[
\theta = 90^\circ
\]

\[
\sin 90^\circ = 1
\]

---

## Calculation

\[
B =
10^{-7}
\cdot
\frac{3 \times 0.1}{(0.2)^2}
\]

\[
B =
10^{-7}
\cdot
\frac{0.3}{0.04}
\]

\[
B = 7.5 \times 10^{-7} \, \text{T}
\]

---

## Final Answer

\[
\boxed{
B = 7.5 \times 10^{-7} \, \text{T}
}
\]
