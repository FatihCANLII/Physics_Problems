Given:
Earth radius R = 6378 km = 6.378 × 10⁶ m  
Angular period T = 24 h = 86400 s  

---

## Step 1: Use centripetal acceleration formula

a_c = v² / R  or  a_c = ω²R  

Using angular velocity:

ω = 2π / T

---

## Step 2: Compute ω

ω = 2π / 86400  ≈ 7.27 × 10⁻⁵ rad/s

---

## Step 3: Compute centripetal acceleration

a_c = ω²R  
    = (7.27 × 10⁻⁵)² × (6.378 × 10⁶)

    ≈ 5.29 × 10⁻⁹ × 6.378 × 10⁶  
    ≈ 0.0337 m/s²

---

## Final Answer

a_c ≈ 0.034 m/s²

(This is much smaller than g ≈ 9.81 m/s²)
