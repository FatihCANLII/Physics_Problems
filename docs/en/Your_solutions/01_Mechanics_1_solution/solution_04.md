r(t) = (3t²)i + (5t - 8t²)j

## Velocity (first derivative)

v(t) = dr/dt

= d/dt(3t²)i + d/dt(5t - 8t²)j
= (6t)i + (5 - 16t)j

---

## Acceleration (second derivative)

a(t) = dv/dt

= d/dt(6t)i + d/dt(5 - 16t)j
= (6)i + (-16)j

---
