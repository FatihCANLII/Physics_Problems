Given:
r(t) = (3t²)i + (5t - 8t²)j

---

## Step 1: Velocity (first derivative)

Velocity is the derivative of position:

v(t) = dr/dt

Differentiate each component:

x(t) = 3t²  ⇒  dx/dt = 6t  
y(t) = 5t - 8t²  ⇒  dy/dt = 5 - 16t  

So:

v(t) = (6t)i + (5 - 16t)j

---

## Step 2: Acceleration (second derivative)

Acceleration is the derivative of velocity:

a(t) = dv/dt

Differentiate again:

d/dt(6t) = 6  
d/dt(5 - 16t) = -16  

So:

a(t) = 6i - 16j

---

## Final Answer

v(t) = (6t)i + (5 - 16t)j  
a(t) = 6i - 16j
