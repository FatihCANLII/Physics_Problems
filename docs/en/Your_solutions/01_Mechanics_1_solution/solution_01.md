...## 1. Projectile Motion

### Given

- Initial speed: v₀ = 100 m/s  
- Launch angle: θ = 37°  
- g = 9.8 m/s²  
- sin(37°) ≈ 0.6, cos(37°) ≈ 0.8  

v₀x = 100 × 0.8 = 80 m/s  
v₀y = 100 × 0.6 = 60 m/s  

---

### Differential Equations of Motion

Horizontal:
d²x/dt² = 0  

Vertical:
d²y/dt² = -g  

---

### Time of Flight

T = (2 v₀y) / g  
T = (2 × 60) / 9.8 ≈ 12.24 s  

---

### Maximum Height

H = (v₀y²) / (2g)  
H = (60²) / (2 × 9.8)  
H = 3600 / 19.6 ≈ 183.67 m  

---

### Range

R = v₀x × T  
R = 80 × 12.24 ≈ 979.2 m
