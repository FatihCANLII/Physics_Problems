Given:
Momentum p = mv

---

## Step 1: Convert masses to kg

Fly: 2 g = 0.002 kg  
Tennis ball: 60 g = 0.06 kg  

---

## Step 2: Calculate momentum

Fly:
p = 0.002 × 10 = 0.02 kg·m/s  

Tennis ball:
p = 0.06 × 1 = 0.06 kg·m/s  

---

## Step 3: Compare

0.06 > 0.02

---

## Final Answer

The **tennis ball** has greater momentum.
