# Section 2: Mechanics II

## 1. Gravitational Dependence

---

## 🔹 Key Formula (Simple Pendulum)

T = 2π √(L / g)

- T: Period  
- L: Length of pendulum  
- g: Gravitational acceleration  

---

## 🟢 Part A: Period on the Moon

### Given:
- Period on Earth: Tₑ = 4 s  
- Gravity on Moon: gₘ = (1/6) gₑ  

---

### Step 1: Relationship

T ∝ 1 / √g

So:

Tₘ / Tₑ = √(gₑ / gₘ)

---

### Step 2: Substitute

Tₘ / 4 = √(gₑ / (gₑ / 6))  
Tₘ / 4 = √6  

Tₘ = 4√6  

---

### Step 3: Final value

√6 ≈ 2.45  

Tₘ ≈ 4 × 2.45 = **9.8 s**

---

### ✅ Answer:
**Period on Moon = 9.8 s**

---

## 🟢 Part B: Length for 1-second Period (Earth)

### Given:
- T = 1 s  
- g = 9.8 m/s²  

---

### Step 1: Rearrange formula

L = gT² / (4π²)

---

### Step 2: Substitute

L = 9.8 × 1² / (4π²)  
L = 9.8 / 39.48  

---

### Step 3: Final value

L ≈ **0.25 m** (25 cm)

---

### ✅ Answer:
**Required length = 0.25 m**

---

## 📌 Summary

| Quantity | Result |
|----------|--------|
| Period on Moon | 9.8 s |
| Length (1 s period) | 0.25 m |
