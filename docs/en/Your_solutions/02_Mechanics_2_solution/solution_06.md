## 6. Energy Dissipation

---

## 🔹 Given

- Initial height: h₀ = 2.0 m  
- Energy loss per bounce: 30%  
→ Energy retained each bounce = 70% = 0.7  

---

## 🟢 Step 1: Key Idea

Mechanical energy is proportional to height:

E ∝ h  

So after each bounce:

h_new = 0.7 × h_previous  

---

## 🟢 Step 2: First Bounce

h₁ = 0.7 × 2.0  
h₁ = **1.4 m**

---

## 🟢 Step 3: Second Bounce

h₂ = 0.7 × 1.4  
h₂ = **0.98 m**

---

## ✅ Final Answer:
**Height after second bounce ≈ 0.98 m**

---

|--------|--------|
