## 2. Harmonic Motion

---

## 🔹 Given Motion

x(t) = 0.2 cos(10π t)

- Mass: m = 10 kg  
- Amplitude: A = 0.2 m  
- Angular frequency: ω = 10π rad/s  

---

## 🟢 Part A: Spring Constant (k)

### Step 1: Use SHM relation

ω = √(k / m)

So:

k = m ω²

---

### Step 2: Substitute values

k = 10 × (10π)²  
k = 10 × 100π²  
k = 1000π²  

---

### Step 3: Final value

π² ≈ 9.87  

k ≈ 1000 × 9.87 = **9870 N/m**

---

### ✅ Answer:
**k ≈ 9.87 × 10³ N/m**

---

## 🟢 Part B: Total Mechanical Energy

### Step 1: Energy formula

E = (1/2) k A²

---

### Step 2: Substitute values

E = (1/2) × 9870 × (0.2)²  
E = (1/2) × 9870 × 0.04  

---

### Step 3: Final value

E ≈ 0.5 × 394.8 = **197.4 J**

---

### ✅ Answer:
**Total energy ≈ 197 J**

---

## 📌 Summary

| Quantity | Result |
|----------|--------|
| Spring constant (k) | 9.87 × 10³ N/m |
| Total energy (E) | 197 J |
