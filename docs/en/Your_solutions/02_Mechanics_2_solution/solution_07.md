## 7. Dynamics with Friction

---

## 🔹 Given

- m1 = 5 kg (top block, tied to wall)  
- m2 = 10 kg (bottom block, moving)  
- Applied force: F = 45 N  
- Coefficient of kinetic friction: μ = 0.2  
- g = 9.8 m/s²  

---

## 🟢 Step 1: Identify Forces on 10 kg Block

Forces opposing motion:

1. Friction with top block  
2. Friction with ground  

---

## 🟢 Step 2: Friction from Top Block

Normal force from top block:

N1 = m1 × g = 5 × 9.8 = 49 N  

Friction:

f1 = μ × N1 = 0.2 × 49 = **9.8 N**

---

## 🟢 Step 3: Friction from Ground

Total normal force on ground:

N2 = (m1 + m2) × g = 15 × 9.8 = 147 N  

Friction:

f2 = μ × N2 = 0.2 × 147 = **29.4 N**

---

## 🟢 Step 4: Net Force on 10 kg Block

F_net = F − f1 − f2  

F_net = 45 − 9.8 − 29.4  
F_net = **5.8 N**

---

## 🟢 Step 5: Acceleration

a = F_net / m2  

a = 5.8 / 10  
a = **0.58 m/s²**

---

## ✅ Final Answer:
**Acceleration ≈ 0.58 m/s²**

---

## 📌 Summary

| Quantity | Value |
|----------|-------|
| Friction (top contact) | 9.8 N |
| Friction (ground) | 29.4 N |
| Net force | 5.8 N |
| Acceleration | 0.58 m/s² |
